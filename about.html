JAWHARAT AUTO Is The Best Place For Your Auto Care

JAWHARAT AL SAHIL AUTO MAINT. has offered superior vehicle denting and painting services for more than twenty-six years. We have built an understanding of our customers and their vehicles. At JAWHARAT AUTO, we go the extra mile for our clients. We don’t only aim to understand what your vehicle requires, but we also aim to understand you as an individual.

With over 26 years of experience in the automotive denting and painting industry, we specialize in restoring vehicles to their original condition, ensuring both aesthetic appeal and structural integrity. Our team of skilled technicians is dedicated to providing high-quality dent repair, painting and collision repair services for all types of vehicles.

Placing your trust in JAWHARAT AUTO will not only give you peace of mind and keep your vehicle on the road but will also provide a worthwhile customer experience.

Our Mission
We aim to deliver exceptional denting and painting services that exceed customer expectations. By combining advanced techniques, premium materials, and a customer-first approach, we ensure that every vehicle leaves our workshop looking as good as new.
Our Vision
To become the most reliable and customer –centric auto denting and painting workshop, offering fast turnaround times, superior workmanship, and cost-effective solutions to vehicle owners and fleet operators.
